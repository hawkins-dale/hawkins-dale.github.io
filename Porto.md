#### Weather

It **will rain**.  Umbrellas are available from street vendors.  If you have an umbrella, it's fun to walk around.

#### Transport
- Porto is **dense**.  Walk everywhere.
- [[Buses in Porto]] are excellent.  Use https://stcp.pt/en/plan-a-trip
- The [[Metro]] is cool: most lines are mostly above ground
- Or ride bikes!  Lots of bike lanes. Or an [e-bike tour](https://www.bajabikes.eu/en/porto-highlights-tour/)!
- Bolt is better than Uber.  It's the local (cheaper) version.
