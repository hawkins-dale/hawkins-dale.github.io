**Serralves** is a cultural institution located in [Porto](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Porto "Porto"), [Portugal](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Portugal "Portugal"). It includes a [contemporary art](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Contemporary_art "Contemporary art") [museum](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Museum "Museum"), a [park](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Park "Park"), and a [villa](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Villa "Villa"), with each one of these being an example of contemporary architecture, [Modernism](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Modernism "Modernism"), and [Art Deco](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Art_Deco "Art Deco") architecture.
